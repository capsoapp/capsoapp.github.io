About Capso

"The innovative treasure exploration platform."

Capso allows you to record funny, beautiful and touching moments as the treasure through video, and attracts friends to the location to explore. You can also follow the treasure map, start your exploration journey to unlock the secrets hidden by your friends.

Capso has two roles, Treasure Creator and Treasure Explorer.

One who preserves and share
the beautiful moments.

Treasure Creator

Creator’s mission is recording the people and the things at the spot through video. Each recorded video will be preserved at the current location as a treasure for friends to explore.

  • Discover beautiful treasure
    Discover beautiful moments and record a 6 to 30 seconds video.
  • Preserve at the current location
    Turn the recorded video into a treasure, and preserve at the current location.
  • Share the treasure map to friends
    Initiate a treasure exploration event. Attract friends to specific locations to explore through treasure clues and map.

One who explores the world, experiences
the touching and beautiful moments.

Treasure Explorer

Explorer can explore the treasure around the world after receiving treasure maps from friends. The more friends you have, the more treasure clues you receive. You can also interact with your friends through likes or comments in Capso.

  • Receive treasure map from friends
    Receive the treasure clues from your friends in real-time.
  • Interact with creator and explore partners
    Interact with creator and explore partners through likes or comments.
  • Explore the treasure through the map
    Follow the treasure map to unlock the secrets of the location, and be surprised!
